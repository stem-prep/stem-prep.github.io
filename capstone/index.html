What is STEM-Prep?

The disciplines of science and math are extraordinarily connected and dependent upon each other. However, many students struggle to apply math skills when they are in science class. STEM-Prep lessons aim to teach students essential math skills that real scientists use every day! Within a lesson students can learn new skills and practice them in the conext of realistic science-based scenarios.
